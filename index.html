📊 Route analyses

Exploratory shape-metrics for each paddle, computed straight from the GPX track. They describe the character of a route — how straight, how wiggly, how it wandered — not who wins. With only a handful of GPS points they're rough and just for fun. The contest is still decided by lowest “avg off”.

🔥 Fire & rest spots

Open fire is legal only in a marked fire-pit at an official site — never on the open bank. 🔴 orange = fire allowed (campsite / laužavietė) · ⚪ grey = rest only (no fire). Most of the route is in Gražutė Regional Park: gather only fallen wood, keep ~25 m from the water, drown the fire fully before leaving. Always check today's fire-danger class — during high danger all open fire is banned. Spots from OpenStreetMap; confirm exact pits with the Gražutė directorate (Salakas).
